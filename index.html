Elon Musk

Elon Musk, a name synonymous with innovation and bold leadership, is the mastermind behind companies such as Tesla, SpaceX, Neuralink, and The Boring Company, pushing the boundaries of what is possible in the fields of sustainable energy and space exploration. He is widely recognized as a visionary and game-changer, constantly challenging the status quo and leaving a lasting impact on the world.

Elon Musk Timeline

  • 1971: Born in Pretoria, South Africa
  • 1995: Studied at University of Pennsylvania, graduated with Physics and Economics degrees
  • 1995-1996: Worked at a software company
  • 1995: Co-founded web software company Zip2
  • 1999: Sold Zip2 to Compaq for 307 million dollars
  • 1999: Co-founded X.com, an online payment company
  • 2002: X.com changed its name to PayPal and went public
  • 2002: PayPal was acquired by eBay for 1.5 billion dollars

  • 2004: Became CEO of Tesla Motors, aimed to produce mass market electric vehicles
